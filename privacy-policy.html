2. Information We Collect

3. How Your Photos and Videos Are Processed

When you upload a photo or video, the following process occurs:

  • Your media is securely uploaded to Google Firebase Storage
  • The file is then sent to Fal.ai for AI processing using Kling AI (video generation) and Google Veo 3 / Imagen (photo generation) models
  • The AI-generated result is returned to the app and stored in Firebase Storage under your device ID
  • You can view, download, or permanently delete your generated content at any time from within the app
  • If you delete your content, it is permanently removed from our servers
Important: Your uploaded photos are used exclusively for generating the AI output you requested. We do not use your photos to train AI models, for advertising, or for any purpose other than delivering your requested result.
